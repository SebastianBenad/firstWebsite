Need a website?

Wait!

If you're the owner of a brand new business & a little tech savy, I recommend searching "wordpress website tutorial" on youtube and copying the first website you like. This saves you $$$ and gets you a website that LOOKS professional. Alternatively most web hosts like goDaddy offer free website builders.

Why hire a Developer?

A website built from scratch & tailored to your needs will have advantages. The most notable are better SEO, faster load speeds & greater freedom when it comes to design. SEO or search engine optimization is a large subject and there are entire teams of people one can hire to get on the front page of google. I don't offer active SEO however, a fast website with proper code goes a long way ;).

Not sure now?

If you're the type of person who wants things done right from the get go & don't mind spending some money to get it done, give me or another developer a shout. ***BUT*** If you plan to frequently edit the site, have a tight budget, or want to become a blogger. Learn wordpress yourself!!! It is not worth it to hire a developer for every little change and if you serve content it's best to know your platform.
